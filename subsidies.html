Government Subsidies for Farmers in India

Explore various schemes and subsidies provided by the Government of India to support farmers.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

A direct income support scheme providing ₹6,000 annually to small and marginal farmers in three equal installments.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A crop insurance scheme offering coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of crop failure due to natural calamities.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan MaanDhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

A pension scheme for small and marginal farmers, providing them with a minimum fixed pension upon reaching the age of 60 years.

Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI) Sub-Scheme

A scheme aimed at creating marketing infrastructure in the agricultural sector to promote better price realization for farmers.

Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres Scheme (ACABC)

A program designed to provide extension services to farmers through qualified agricultural professionals, enhancing farm productivity and income.
