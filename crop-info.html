Crop Information

Discover crops grown in India during different seasons and their key details.

Kharif Crops

Kharif crops are grown during the monsoon season and harvested in autumn.

Rice

Regions: Punjab, Haryana, West Bengal

Season: June - October

Maize

Regions: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Bihar

Season: June - October

Jute

Regions: West Bengal, Assam, Bihar

Season: June - September

Rabi Crops

Rabi crops are grown during winter and harvested in spring.

Wheat

Regions: Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh

Season: November - April

Mustard

Regions: Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana

Season: October - February

Barley

Regions: Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh

Season: November - April

Zaid Crops

Zaid crops are grown between Rabi and Kharif seasons in summer.

Watermelon

Regions: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu

Season: March - June

Cucumber

Regions: West Bengal, Odisha, Bihar

Season: March - June

Moong Dal

Regions: Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh

Season: March - May
